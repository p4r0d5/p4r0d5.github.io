* new cert only
```
certbot certonly -d domain --apache --agree-tos -m myemailaddress@mydomain.com

Certificate is saved at: /etc/letsencrypt/live/domain/fullchain.pem
Key is saved at:         /etc/letsencrypt/live/domain/privkey.pem
```
